Sebastián Freille

I am an academic economist. I teach and do research in public economics and political economy. I am based at the Universidad Nacional de Córdoba (UNC). My interests are in sub-national elections, political economy of redistribution, special interests groups, lobbying and corruption. I am and will always be a Maradonian.

Research Interests

  • Public Economics and Public Finance
  • Political Economy
  • Sub-national Elections and Electoral Politics
  • Political Economy of Redistribution
  • Special Interest Groups, Lobbying and Influence
  • Corruption and Institutional Quality
